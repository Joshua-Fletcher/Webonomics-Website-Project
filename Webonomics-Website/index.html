Our Vision

Community

We strive to create a community driven towards engagement and transparency with our users. Our platforms cover a wide demographic of discussion topics to accommodate for all types of user interests and hobbies.

Airdrop

Our objective with the airdrop is to encourage users to be proactive in our community Discord, Twitter, and Reddit. This provides a unique opportunity for our members to partake in our communities and receive access to our whitelist.

Learn More

Application

Creating decentralized applications and platforms for everyday users to embark on and engage with the world is the team's main goal. The overwhelming majority of social media platforms track and store user’s data. The environment in the platforms we create won’t instill any fear of data tracking or storage of private information. It is complete freedom to the user.
