Our Vision

Community

Our community will be driven towards engagement and transparency with our users. The platforms cover a wide demographic of discussion topics to accommodate every type of user’s interests and hobbies.

Airdrop

The airdrop will encourage users to be proactive in our community Discord, Twitter, and Reddit which, in turn, will provide a unique opportunity for our members to partake in our communities and receive access to our whitelist.

Learn More

Application

Creating decentralized applications and platforms for everyday users to embark on and engage with the world is the team's main goal. The overwhelming majority of social media platforms track and store user’s data. The environment in the platforms we create won’t instill any fear of data tracking or storage of private information. It is complete freedom to the user.

Get Involved

Part of our community engagement outside of social media and discussions are the events and giveaways. These events vary from weekly hot topics discussion boards to gaming tournaments held our users who take initiative. There are no limits to these events and we encourage our members to create and lead their own events through our community.

Community WEB is designed to inspire members to partake in discussion and create unique conversation between like-minded individuals within our environment.
